  • Development

    • Reele, reele.co

      Reele is a platform for audio professionals to showcase their work and connect with other audiophiles. Built with a 4-person team using Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML, SCSS, AJAX, Scraping, Cloudinary, and ActiveRecord. Our team utilized GitHub throughout the development process and hosted Reele on Heroku. Features include audio upload, audio and video embed, personal public profiles, dashboard with private and public states of projects, messaging, and social media currencies (views, likes, follows).
